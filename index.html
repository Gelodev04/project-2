What is Discuss that Coin?

Every day, thousands of new tokens are launched. But too often, their communities are gated, heavily moderated, or even silencing members who speak the truth.

Discuss That Coin changes that.

Our platform creates a decentralized, open space for each token—where anyone can share honest opinions without fear of being banned or censored.

It's about real feedback, from real people. No filters. No fear. Just truth from the community itself.
